HARI - HAr GhaR GIta

Bhagawad Gita is the essence of all Upanishadic knowledge encapsulated to cater to the welfare of the mankind across eons, through selfless service, devotion and knowledge. Parama Pujya Sri Ganapathy Sachchidananda Swamiji, during the first consecration celebrations (Kumbhabhishekam) of Kārya Siddhi Hanuman Temple in August 2015, emphasized the importance of Bhagawad Gita and instructed everyone to study Gita. Following HIS command 175+ students of ages 5-49 memorized the entire Bhagawad Gita of 18 chapters consisting of 700 shlokas. As a humble tribute to their Sadguru Pujya Sri Swamiji, these kids have formed into groups and have taken a resolve (HARI - HAr GhaR GIta) to spread the importance and benefits of Srimad Bhagawad Gita Parayana to various households and organizations

Highlights

Bhagawad Gita is highly beneficial in both the material and in the spiritual lives. Gita is the prime most amongst the spiritual works available for spiritual seekers. Padma Purana states that while it requires a certain caliber to chant the Bhagawad Gita knowing its meaning, even simply chanting the verses also produces great rewards. The Bhagawad Gita in essence, is a treasure house of powerful sound. Regular chanting of the Bhagawad Gita develops a good personal culture. It bestows great comforts and life becomes fulfilled.
