Mission

Our mission is to make the epic Srimad Bhagavad Gita accessible to everyone. Following His Holiness Sri Ganapathy Sachchidananda Swamiji’s word, nearly 200 people have memorized the entire Bhagavad Gita in a span of 10 months over the last 2 years (as of 2017). We want to take this successful method and model of learning to everyone that may be interested, so everyone may benefit from the divine guidance of the Bhagavad Gita.

This site has been created by followers of His Holiness Sri Ganapathy Sachchidananda Swamiji to enable a wider audience to gain access to tools that have been successful in memorizing the entire Bhagavad Gita. The site will provide tutorials for you to memorize or read the Bhagavad Gita shlokas. You also have the divine opportunity to be part the annual Maha Yajna each year where everyone that is learning the Bhagavad Gita within the 10-month period will come together and chant the Bhagavad Gita in unison in the divine presence of His Holiness Sri Ganapathy Sachchidananda Swamiji. Maha Yagna

The Bhagavad Gita Parayana Maha Yajna program is an annual program where children ages 5 and up, as well as adults memorize the entire Bhagavad Gita by heart. At the end of 10 months, the program will culminate in a Bhagavad Gita Maha Yajna where all participants will have the opportunity to come together to recite the holy Gita from memory in the divine presence of His Holiness Sri Ganapathy Sachchidanand Swamiji. This transformative program has had nearly 200 successful participants in the two years since inception in 2015. Participants in this program will be taught the precise pronunciation of each verse in the Bhagavad Gita. The program with its unique model of classroom and online teaching is growing very fast. There are nearly 20 centers across the United States of America. There are also centers in other countries including Canada, India, Trinidad, Kuwait etc."

Inspired by Pujya Sri Swamiji’s call to study and memorize Bhagavad Gita, children from USA took it as a Deeksha to study and memorize entire Bhagavad Gita and accomplished it in mere 10 months.

43 children of ages 7-17 sang Sampoorna Parayana for the first time in Karya Siddhi Hanuman Temple Dallas on 17th July 2016.

Greatly pleased with their efforts, Pujya Sri Swamiji invited the kids to visit Datta Peetham and offered the Parayana of Sampoorna Bhagavad Gita in Nada Mantapa on 25th December 2016

As a blessing to the young Parayanakartas Pujya Sri Swamiji took them on a pilgrimage to the sacred land of Kurukshetra in Haryana and chanted Bhagavad Gita on 28th Dec 2016.

Batch#2 111 kids and 23 adults ages from 5 - 49 sang Sampoorna Parayana in Nada Mantapa on July 16th 2017.

HARI - HAr GhaR GIta

Bhagawad Gita is the essence of all Upanishadic knowledge encapsulated to cater to the welfare of the mankind across eons, through selfless service, devotion and knowledge. Parama Pujya Sri Ganapathy Sachchidananda Swamiji, during the first consecration celebrations (Kumbhabhishekam) of Kārya Siddhi Hanuman Temple in August 2015, emphasized the importance of Bhagawad Gita and instructed everyone to study Gita. Following HIS command 175+ students of ages 5-49 memorized the entire Bhagawad Gita of 18 chapters consisting of 700 shlokas. As a humble tribute to their Sadguru Pujya Sri Swamiji, these kids have formed into groups and have taken a resolve (HARI - HAr GhaR GIta) to spread the importance and benefits of Srimad Bhagawad Gita Parayana to various households and organizations

Highlights

The Bhagawad Gita in essence, is a treasure house of powerful sound. Regular chanting of the Bhagawad Gita develops a good personal culture. It bestows great comforts and life becomes fulfilled.
