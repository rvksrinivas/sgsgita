DALLAS CENTER

Continuing the divine vision and sankalpa of Parama Pujya Sri Ganapathy Sachchidananda Swamiji of Avadhoota Datta Peetham, new batch orientation for Gita Mahayagna will commence on August 10th. The goal is to have the kids and adults memorize all 18 chapters (700 Verses) by end of May next year. As indicated in the registration form only adults have an option for fluent reading. This will definitely be an intensive program, but by lord's and Sadguru's blessings and grace and with sincere efforts, participants can be assured of sure success.
